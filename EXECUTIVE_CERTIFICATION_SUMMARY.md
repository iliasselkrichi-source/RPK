# Executive Certification Summary

## Status

**FLEETCONNECT PRODUCTION CERTIFIED**

FleetConnect has completed final production certification and founder live testing. The platform is ready for production use within the certified scope.

## Platform Summary

FleetConnect is a production transport dispatch and booking platform with customer booking, customer portal, operator dashboard, partner/driver workflows, Partner PWA, multilingual support, review system, lifecycle email automation, Stripe sandbox payment validation, Supabase backend, and production deployment.

## Certified Outcomes

- Customers can book, register, verify, log in, and manage their booking context.
- Operators can manage bookings, customers, partners, drivers, account requests, assignments, and reviews.
- Partners and drivers can access the PWA and execute assigned ride workflows.
- Emails are routed through the certified FleetConnect lifecycle automation.
- Reviews persist and display through the validated review workflow.
- Supabase schema, RPCs, and access model support the certified workflows.
- Stripe sandbox checkout and webhook behavior have been validated.

## Final Recommendation

FleetConnect is approved for production operation.
